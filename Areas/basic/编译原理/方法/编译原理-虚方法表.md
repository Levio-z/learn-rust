## 1. 核心观点  
### Ⅰ. 概念层

支持[动态分派](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dynamic_dispatch "Dynamic dispatch") （或[运行时](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Run_time_\(program_lifecycle_phase\) "Run time (program lifecycle phase)")方法[绑定](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Name_binding "Name binding") ）的一种机制。

每当一个[类](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Class_\(programming\) "Class (programming)")定义了[虚函数](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Virtual_function "Virtual function") （或[虚方法](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Method_\(computer_programming\) "Method (computer programming)") ）时，大多数[编译器](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Compiler "Compiler")都会向该类添加一个隐藏的[成员变量](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Member_variable "Member variable") ，该变量指向一个包含指向（虚）函数的[指针](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pointer_\(computer_programming\))数组，称为虚方法表。这些指针在运行时用于调用相应的函数实现，因为在编译时可能还不知道要调用的是基类函数还是继[承自基](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Inheritance_\(object-oriented_programming\) "Inheritance (object-oriented programming)")类的派生类实现的派生函数。
